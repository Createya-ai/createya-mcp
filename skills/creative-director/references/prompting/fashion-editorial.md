# Fashion editorial — Vogue / i-D / CR Fashion Book стандарт

Главная формула для высококлассной модной съёмки. Цель — образ, который рассказывает историю, не просто показывает одежду.

## Принцип
> "Editorial — это точка зрения. Каждый кадр должен иметь режиссёрское решение, а не быть красивой картинкой с одеждой."

Отличие от ecommerce: здесь приветствуются dynamic poses, dramatic light, artistic composition, strong mood.

## Locked composition (editorial)

```
Setting: [color cyclorama / architectural location / outdoor editorial setting].
Framing: full body model occupying 75-85% of vertical frame.
         Head slightly below top edge, feet with 5-10% breathing room at bottom.
Lighting: [see lighting options below — NOT even fill].
Camera: Sony A7R IV 35mm full-frame or Hasselblad 907X.
Angle: primary — frontal eye-level for hero; 3/4 and low-angle for drama.
Model energy: dynamic, directional, intentional — NOT neutral ecommerce stance.
```

## Варианты освещения (выбирать под mood)

### Option A — Soft three-point editorial
```
Three-point lighting: large key softbox (left or right, 45°), fill reflector opposite side
at 50% power, rim/hair light from behind creating separation.
Clean, professional, versatile. For: beauty-forward editorial, luxury fashion.
```

### Option B — Low-key chiaroscuro
```
Single directional key light (45-60°), no fill, dramatic shadow on opposite side,
1:4 key-to-shadow ratio. For: dark/moody editorial, avant-garde, menswear.
```

### Option C — Golden hour outdoor
```
Warm directional sunlight at 15° above horizon (golden hour), backlit halo on hair,
skin glowing, fill from reflector or ambient. For: romantic, summer, resort editorial.
```

### Option D — Blue hour / mixed practical
```
Twilight ambient (blue-grey sky), practical lights in environment (street lamps,
window glow), high ISO grain permitted. For: urban editorial, street-inspired.
```

[From options above — select and expand].

## Позы для editorial вариаций

Не нейтральные ecommerce-позы. Использовать:

**Power poses:**
- "dominant power stance, weight forward, shoulders squared at camera"
- "contrapposto, one hip pushed sideways, weight on back foot"

**Movement:**
- "walking confidently toward camera, mid-stride, hair in motion"
- "spinning 3/4 turn, fabric flowing outward, caught mid-movement"

**Gestural:**
- "one hand touching collar lightly, other arm extended, fingers splayed"
- "both hands in pockets, relaxed slump, direct eye contact"

**Mood:**
- "seated on floor, knees up, arms wrapped around knees, pensive"
- "leaning against wall, arms crossed, looking slightly off-camera"

## Цветовые palettes по mood

| Mood | Background | Palette | Вариант |
|------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Clean luxury | White cyclorama | Neutral, accurate | Soft three-point |
| Dark drama | Black void / dark location | Muted dark | Low-key chiaroscuro |
| Warm romance | Warm tan / terracotta wall | Warm earth tones | Golden hour |
| Urban edge | Concrete / grafitti / city | Desaturated with pop | Blue hour + practical |
| Colour pop | Bold saturated cyclorama | Graphic, high contrast | Hard light |
