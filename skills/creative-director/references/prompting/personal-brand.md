# Personal brand — founder, expert, инфлюенсер

Два чётко разграниченных стрима для personal brand фотосессий. Смешивать нельзя — они служат разным целям.

## Принцип
> "Corporate — доверие и авторитет. Creative — личность и история. Оба нужны. Выбор зависит от канала публикации."

## Stream A — Corporate professional

**Для**: LinkedIn, website, корпоративные презентации, B2B материалы, медиа-кит.

### Locked composition (corporate)

```
Background: light grey seamless (#E8E8E8) or soft gradient white-to-grey.
Framing: waist-up (bust shot), person occupies 55-65% of frame.
         Slight breathing room top, centered horizontally.
Lighting: soft three-point studio setup —
          large softbox key (45° left), fill reflector (opposite, 70% key power),
          optional hair light from behind.
          Result: clean, flattering, zero harsh shadows.
Camera: Sony A7R IV, 85mm f/2.8, focus on eyes, shallow DOF blurring background.
Pose: standing straight OR slight angled stance (10-15°), shoulders relaxed,
      arms: one hand in pocket or both loosely at sides.
Expression: confident, approachable, direct gaze.
```

### Prompt add-ons для corporate

```
"Professional headshot quality, business-appropriate styling,
polished but not stiff, approachable expertise.
Subtle hint of personality while maintaining professional credibility."
```

**Одежда**: business casual → smart casual. Цвет: navy, charcoal, white, мuted earth tones. Избегать: яркие принты, sportswear, casual hoodies.

## Stream B — Creative / intimate

**Для**: Instagram, личный блог, подкаст-обложки, нативная реклама, storytelling content.

### Locked composition (creative)

```
Background: natural environment — workspace (desk with items), coffee shop, outdoor,
            home studio, creative space. Environment reveals character.
Framing: various — full body 3/4, environmental portrait (person in space),
         tight face + hands, overhead lifestyle.
Lighting: natural window light OR ambient practical lights.
          Window light: "soft diffused daylight from left window, natural falloff".
          Evening practical: "warm tungsten desk lamp, soft ambient, slight bokeh".
Camera: 50mm f/1.8 (for intimacy/depth) or 35mm f/2.8 (environmental).
Pose: relaxed, mid-activity — typing, reading, holding coffee, mid-gesture explaining.
Expression: candid, genuine, in-the-moment — NOT posed smile.
```

### Prompt add-ons для creative

```
"Environmental portrait: character revealed through surroundings.
Authentic, candid quality — caught in their element.
Warm lifestyle energy, intimate connection with viewer."
```

**Одежда**: широкий диапазон — от casual к smart casual в зависимости от ниши.

## Когда какой стрим использовать

| Канал / Use case | Stream | Примечание |
|-----------------|--------|-----------|
| LinkedIn profile photo | A (corporate) | Hero front, tight crop |
| Website "About" hero | A или A+B mix | A для trust, B для personality |
| Instagram feed | B (creative) | Lifestyle, environmental |
| Podcast cover | B | Face close, editorial |
| Press release / media | A (corporate) | Formal, clean |
| YouTube thumbnail | B | Expressive, direct eye contact |
| Book/course cover | Зависит от темы | Corporate ниша → A, Creative ниша → B |
| Instagram Stories | B | Candid, in-the-moment |
| Company "Team" page | A (corporate) | Consistent across team |

## Mood adjustments

### Corporate mood dial

| Dial | Less formal | More formal |
|------|------------|------------|
| Background | Warm grey | Cool white |
| Pose | Angled, one hand in pocket | Straight, both hands visible |
| Expression | Slight hint of smile | Composed confidence |
| Lens | 85mm f/2.8 (warmer DOF) | 85mm f/5.6 (crisper) |

### Creative mood dial

| Dial | Casual/relatable | Thought leader |
|------|-----------------|----------------|
| Environment | Kitchen, outdoor | Office/studio |
| Light | Bright natural | Moody practical |
| Activity | Coffee, casual | Working, writing |
| Lens | 35mm (wide, immersive) | 50mm (intimate) |

---

## Серия для цельного media kit (рекомендуемый порядок)

1. **Hero corporate** (A-stream) — white bg, 85mm, waist-up → используется везде
2. **Environmental portrait** (B-stream) — person in workspace → website
3. **Action/process shot** (B-stream) — person doing their work → content
4. **Close face editorial** (B-stream) — tight face shot, natural light → podcasts/media
5. **Full body casual** (B-stream) → Instagram/social

Эталон делается по A-stream (corporate hero), B-stream кадры — вариации.
