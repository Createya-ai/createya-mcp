# Product hero — товар без человека

Чистая предметная съёмка в двух вариантах: studio clean и elemental dramatic. Без модели, без рук, только товар.

## Принцип
> "Студийный — для доверия и продаж. Элементальный — для эмоции и брендинга. Выбор определяет медиа-канал."

---

## Вариант A — Clean studio (ecommerce premium)

**Для**: маркетплейсы premium tier, бренд-сайт, каталог, онлайн-магазин.

### Locked composition (clean studio)

```
Background: pure white (#FFFFFF) or very light neutral grey (#F5F5F5).
Framing: product occupies 70-80% of canvas, fully in frame, slight breathing room.
Lighting: tent lighting (all-around diffused) OR top-down soft fill + side softbox.
          Goal: even exposure, no harsh shadows, material color accurate.
Camera: 100mm macro f/8-f/11 (for small items — jewellery, cosmetics, tech accessories)
        or 50mm f/8 (for larger items — bags, shoes, bottles).
Angle: frontal eye-level (hero) or slight 3/4 for showing depth/form.
Surface: white seamless or light matte surface (no mirror, no glass reflections).
```

### Shot list (5 базовых)

| # | Shot | Угол | Lens | Цель |
|---|------|------|------|------|
| 1 | Hero front | Frontal eye-level | 100mm | Главный идентификатор |
| 2 | 3/4 structural | 30° turn + 15° above | 100mm | Объём и форма |
| 3 | Top-down | Directly overhead | 50mm | Flat lay / layout |
| 4 | Side profile | 90° | 100mm | Толщина, профиль |
| 5 | Macro detail | Extreme close | 100mm macro | Текстура, материал, логотип |

### Prompt template (clean studio)

```
Professional product photography of [PRODUCT]: [description — material, dimensions,
color, key features, distinctive elements].

Pure white seamless background, studio environment.
Product positioned [angle from shot list], fills 75% of frame.
Tent lighting setup: soft diffused light from all sides, even exposure,
no cast shadows on background, material colors accurate and true-to-life.
Subtle base shadow beneath product for grounding only (not background shadow).

Camera: Sony A7R IV, [100mm macro / 50mm] lens, f/[8-11], ISO 100, tripod mounted.
Focus: edge-to-edge sharpness throughout product.

Quality: commercial product photography, photorealistic, high detail surface texture,
accurate color reproduction, 8K resolution.

Negative: background shadows, gradients, props, hands, dramatic lighting, motion blur,
color correction, reflections from background, vignette, artistic effects.
```

## Вариант B — Elemental dramatic (ad / luxury / campaign)

**Для**: рекламные кампании, luxury brand, социальные сети (hero post), OOH.

### Стили elemental

**Dark void / edge light:**
```
Background: deep black void, near-black dark grey.
Lighting: twin edge lights (both sides, 90°) OR single edge light with hard falloff.
Effect: product appears to float in darkness, edges glow, material character revealed.
Best for: watches, spirits, perfume, dark-toned products.
```

**Water / liquid / mist:**
```
Background: dark or mid-tone, product partially surrounded by water splash / mist.
Lighting: backlit or side-lit for transmission through liquid.
Effect: dynamic, energetic, freshness signal.
Best for: beverages, skincare, supplements, cleaning products.
```

**Surface reflection (luxury slab):**
```
Background: dark polished stone (marble, granite) or mirror.
Lighting: edge light creating perfect product reflection below.
Effect: prestige, premium, architectural.
Best for: perfume, spirits, premium cosmetics, jewellery.
```

**Natural elemental:**
```
Background: raw natural material — dark wet concrete, rough stone, aged wood.
Lighting: single strong side light, hard shadows, dramatic.
Effect: craft, authenticity, raw quality.
Best for: spirits, artisan food, leather goods, natural cosmetics.
```

### Prompt template (elemental dramatic)

```
[Elemental style descriptor: "dark void product shot" / "water splash product photography" /
"luxury stone slab product photography" / "raw natural texture product photography"]

[PRODUCT DESCRIPTION — 3 sentences: name, material, key visual features].

[ELEMENTAL ENVIRONMENT — 3 sentences: background type, surface, surrounding elements].

[LIGHTING — 3 sentences from elemental style options above].

Camera: Sony A7R IV, 50mm f/4 (editorial feel) or 100mm macro f/8 (detail focus).
[Any camera movement note: static / slight tilt if applicable].

Mood: [dramatic / powerful / refined / raw / energetic].
Style: advertising campaign photography, cinematic, brand campaign quality.
Quality: photorealistic, ultra-high detail, 8K, fine art print quality.

Negative: white studio background, flat even lighting, casual consumer-grade feel,
no text, no watermark, no hands.
```

## Decision tree: A или B?

```
Product category → channel → variant:

Маркетплейс listing?         → A (clean studio)
Brand website hero?          → B (elemental) если luxury, иначе A
Social media campaign post?  → B (elemental)
Print advertising?           → B (elemental)
Email newsletter product?    → A (clean studio, light version)
Luxury brand DTC?            → B (dark/stone depending on product)
Spirits / fragrance?         → B (dark void или stone slab)
Mass market FMCG?            → A (clean white, accurate colors)
```

## Комбинированный workflow для brand

Эталон делается по варианту A (clean studio, frontal). Рекламные кадры — отдельная сессия по варианту B. Они не вариации друг друга — это разные цели.
