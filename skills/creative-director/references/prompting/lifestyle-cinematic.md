# Lifestyle cinematic — narrative lifestyle с кинематографичным mood

Story-driven lifestyle фотография. Персонаж в мире, который его характеризует. Атмосфера важнее технической точности.

## Принцип
> "Каждый кадр — стоп-кадр из фильма, который существует за пределами этого кадра. Location reveals character. Light builds atmosphere."

Не UGC, не editorial — это visual storytelling. Зритель должен захотеть оказаться там.

---

## Три narrative streams

### Stream 1 — Warm welcoming (warmth, connection, joy)

```
Tone markers: "warm inviting atmosphere, golden light, human warmth,
joy and connection, approachable lifestyle"

Light: warm golden hour (outdoor) / warm practical interior (candles, pendants)
Palette: amber, honey, warm cream, terracotta accents
Emotion: "genuine happiness, relaxed openness, warmth"
```

Типичные сцены: friends at table, morning coffee ritual, family moment, casual celebration.

### Stream 2 — Cool moody (mystery, depth, sophistication)

```
Tone markers: "cool sophisticated atmosphere, editorial mood, quiet depth,
contemplative, slightly melancholic beauty"

Light: blue hour exterior / cold practical lights / overcast diffused
Palette: desaturated blues, slate grey, deep green, cool neutrals
Emotion: "pensive gaze, quiet confidence, solitude by choice"
```

Типичные сцены: urban solitary, rain-wet street, minimal apartment evening, reading alone.

### Stream 3 — Vibrant energetic (motion, life, momentum)

```
Tone markers: "energetic dynamic atmosphere, forward momentum, vibrant life,
spontaneous energy, joyful movement"

Light: bright midday / mixed sunlight / dynamic practical
Palette: saturated brights, high contrast, vivid pops
Emotion: "laughing mid-motion, caught mid-action, unstoppable energy"
```

Типичные сцены: outdoor adventure, market/street, running/cycling, group energy.

## Location — как location reveals character

Character trait → Location choice:
- Intellectual / thoughtful → library, bookshop, minimal study, museum
- Creative / artistic → studio space, art gallery, vintage shop
- Active / healthy → park morning, running path, outdoor cafe
- Successful / ambitious → modern office, city rooftop, contemporary interior
- Warm / communal → kitchen, family table, neighbourhood cafe
- Urban / sophisticated → city street, modern bar, architectural interior
- Natural / free → forest path, beach, mountain terrain

**Правило**: не выбирать "красивое" место — выбирать место, которое что-то говорит о персонаже.

## Lighting guide по времени суток

### Golden hour (sunset/sunrise)
```
Camera settings hint: "warm directional sunlight at 15° above horizon,
golden backlit halo on hair and shoulders, skin glowing warm,
long soft shadows on ground, fill from reflector or ambient bounce."
Color: 3200-3500K, amber/gold tones.
Best for: Stream 1 (warm welcoming), romantic content.
```

### Blue hour (20 min after sunset)
```
"Twilight ambient: blue-grey sky as soft light source, practical lights beginning
to glow warm in background, high-contrast sky/warm-interior relationship,
slight underexposure adds mystery."
Color: 6500K+ ambient, 3200K practicals = mixed color tension.
Best for: Stream 2 (cool moody), urban scenes.
```

### Mixed practical (interior evening)
```
"Warm tungsten interior lights (pendant lamps, floor lamps, candles) as practical sources,
mixed color temperature pools of light, natural shadows between light zones,
ISO-grain permitted for atmosphere."
Best for: intimate interior scenes, Stream 1 + 2.
```

### Overcast diffused (neutral)
```
"Soft grey overcast sky as giant diffuser, even shadow-free light,
no harsh shadows, slightly muted colors, documentary quality."
Best for: Stream 2 and 3, versatile.
```

## Camera по mood

| Stream | Lens | Aperture | Note |
|--------|------|----------|------|
| Warm welcoming | 35mm | f/2.0-2.8 | Wide, immersive, inclusive |
| Cool moody | 50mm | f/1.8-2.8 | Standard, intimate |
| Vibrant energetic | 24-35mm | f/4-5.6 | Wide to capture motion context |
| All environmental | 35mm f/2.8 | f/2.8 | Best balance of DOF + sharpness |
| Character close | 85mm f/2 | f/2-2.8 | Shallow, cinematic portrait feel |

## Полный prompt template (280+ слов)

```
[NARRATIVE CONCEPT — 2 sentences]:
[What moment this is: "a quiet morning before the city wakes" /
"the first evening back home after a long trip" / "an unplanned afternoon turning golden"].
[Who this person is in this world].

[CHARACTER — 3-4 sentences from character sheet OR new]:
[appearance, styling appropriate to scene — NOT studio styling, real-life dressing].

[LOCATION — 3 sentences]:
[Specific setting — detailed, not generic: not "coffee shop" but 
"small neighbourhood espresso bar, worn wooden counter, steamed-up windows"].
[What makes this location feel real and specific].
[Depth of field context: foreground / midground / background relationship].

[MOMENT / ACTION — 3-4 sentences]:
[What exactly is happening — specific not generic].
[Body language: weight, hands, posture — in-the-moment not posed].
[Expression: what they're feeling, not performing].
[Motion if any: static stillness / subtle movement / mid-action].

[LIGHTING — 3-4 sentences]:
[Time of day, source from mood streams above].
[How light falls specifically on character].
[Shadow direction and quality].
[Atmospheric light elements: lens flare / bokeh balls / light dust / mist].

Camera: Sony A7R IV or Canon EOS R5, [focal length from table],
f/[aperture], [1/250 freeze motion / 1/60 slight blur].
[Any cinematic treatment: "Kodak Vision3 500T film emulation" if desired].

Tone: [from stream options above].
Color treatment: [warm/cool/vibrant palette note from stream].
Style: cinematic lifestyle photography, narrative still, photorealistic.
Quality: cinematic quality, high detail, film-like rendering, 8K.

Negative: staged look, obvious posing, stock photo feel,
[stream-specific: "no cold lighting" for warm / "no harsh fill" for moody],
no text, no watermark.
```

## Series planning для lifestyle shoot

Рекомендуемая структура серии (8-10 кадров):

1. **Establishing shot** — персонаж в пространстве, мало crop (35mm, f/5.6+)
2. **Environmental portrait** — medium (50-85mm, f/2.8)
3. **Activity closeup** — деталь действия (100mm macro)
4. **Face moment** — эмоция (85mm, f/2.0)
5. **Over-shoulder** — POV из мира героя
6. **Detail** — предметы, которые рассказывают историю
7-10. **Variations** — изменение ракурса, момента, distance
